# Morse Alerts — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Morse Alerts is designed to respect your privacy. The app does **not collect, store, or transmit personal data externally**. All processing is performed locally on your device.

---

## 2. Information the App Accesses

Morse Alerts requires access to certain system features in order to function:

- **Notifications**  
  The app reads notification content (such as title and message text) to convert them into Morse code alerts.

- **Vibration / Audio**  
  Used to deliver Morse code output.

- **Connected devices (e.g. Wear OS watch)**  
  Notification data may be transmitted to a paired watch device to provide alerts.

---

## 3. Data Storage

- A **temporary message log** may be stored locally on your device for debugging or user reference purposes.
- This log is:
  - Stored **only on your device**
  - Automatically deleted after **up to 24 hours**
- No long-term storage of notification content is performed.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Morse Alerts does **not share any data with external servers or third parties**.

Data may only be transmitted:
- **Between your phone and a paired wearable device** (e.g. a Wear OS watch)

No data is sent over the internet or to external services.

---

## 5. Personal Data

- Morse Alerts does **not collect personal information**
- No account or login is required
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used

---

## 6. Security

All data processing occurs locally on your device.  
The app does not transmit or store data externally, reducing exposure to third-party risks.

---

## 7. Your Control

You can control or disable app functionality at any time by:

- Revoking notification access
- Uninstalling the app
- Disabling features within app settings

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Morse Alerts is not specifically targeted at children and does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age.
